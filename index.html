What is Cortex

Not a wiki. Not a search tool.
A brain that thinks.

Most product teams suffer from the same problem: context dies at every handoff. Engineers forget why things were built. PMs write specs blind. New hires take months to onboard.

Cortex is an AI-native product knowledge graph that ingests your entire development artifact stack — code, PRDs, GitHub PRs, Notion docs — and synthesises it into structured, queryable memory.

Not a document store. Not a chat over your files. A living model of your product that knows what exists, why it was built, what's at risk, and what's missing.
