Use cases

Built for everyone
in your org.

Not just for engineers. Cortex gives every role the context they need — in the AI tools they already use.

Stop speccing in the dark.

Your AI tools don't know what your team has already built, decided, or shipped. Cortex changes that — so every spec, every feature decision, every intake review is grounded in reality.

  • Ask "what did we decide about offline mode?" and get the actual answer — with source
  • Generate specs pre-filled with existing architecture and constraints
  • Instantly know if a feature request was already built, rejected, or in progress
  • Understand the why behind every product decision — not just what exists
Brain query · PM
→ "Why was the checkout flow split into two steps?"
Decision found in PR #89 (Mar 2024)
Checkout was split to reduce drop-off at payment entry. A/B test (n=4,200) showed 23% lift in completion rate with the two-step flow.

Source: Notion PRD v3 · GitHub PR #89 · Mar 2024

Accurate answers about your codebase.

Generic AI answers aren't enough when you're working in a complex, evolving system. Cortex gives your AI tools the org-specific context to answer questions about your actual implementation — not just patterns.

  • Ask how any module works and get answers specific to your codebase
  • Understand what breaks before you touch anything
  • Onboard in days instead of weeks — Brain knows your architecture
  • Trace every decision back to the PR or doc that created it
